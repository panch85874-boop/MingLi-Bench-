📱 手機 / 雲端列印Mobile / Cloud Print

我要印手機裡的檔案Print a file from my phone
好的，請拿手機掃描機台上的 QR Code，就能上傳檔案列印。Scan the QR code on the machine to upload and print.
QR Code 在哪裡Where is the QR code?
QR Code 在機台螢幕或機身貼紙上，掃描後手機會打開上傳頁面。The QR code is on the screen or a sticker; scanning opens the upload page.
支援什麼檔案格式What file formats are supported?
支援 PDF 與 Word、Excel、PPT 等 Office 檔，免轉檔直接列印；也支援 JPG、PNG 圖片。單檔上限 150MB。We support PDF and Office files (Word, Excel, PPT)—no conversion needed—plus JPG/PNG images. Max 150MB per file.
LINE 的檔案怎麼印How do I print a file from LINE?
先在 LINE 把檔案存到手機，再掃 QR Code 從上傳頁選那個檔案。Save the file from LINE first, then upload after scanning the QR code.
我要印照片I want to print a photo
掃 QR Code 後上傳照片，可選彩色與尺寸列印於一般紙張；本店不提供相片紙與照片沖印。Scan the QR, upload your photo, pick color and size to print on plain paper; we don't offer photo paper or photo developing.
要選黑白還是彩色Black-and-white or color?
上傳後在手機頁面可切換黑白或彩色，彩色費用較高。Switch black-and-white or color on your phone; color costs more.
怎麼設定份數How do I set the number of copies?
在手機預覽頁面可調整份數，確認後再付款。Set the number of copies on the preview page, then pay.
可以雙面印嗎Can I print double-sided?
可以，在手機設定頁面選「雙面列印」即可。Yes—choose double-sided in the settings.
紙張大小怎麼選How do I choose paper size?
可選 A4 或 A3，在手機頁面的紙張設定切換。Choose A4 or A3 under paper settings.
印之前可以先看嗎Can I preview before printing?
可以，付款前手機會顯示預覽，確認沒問題再付款。Yes—you'll see a preview before paying.
檔案太大傳不上去My file is too big to upload
檔案較大請稍等上傳完成，或先壓縮、分次上傳。For large files, wait or compress and split.
上傳後找不到檔案Can't find my file after uploading
請確認上傳完成的提示，沒有的話重新掃 QR Code 再上傳一次。Check the upload-complete prompt; if missing, rescan and upload again.
上傳完怎麼付款How do I pay after uploading?
確認預覽後點付款，依畫面用電子支付或掃碼完成即可。After the preview, tap pay and complete via mobile payment.
印好的紙在哪裡拿Where do I collect my prints?
付款完成後紙張會從出紙匣送出，請到出紙口拿取。Collect your prints from the output tray after payment.
可以一次印很多檔案嗎Can I print many files at once?
可以，上傳頁面能加入多個檔案，會一起計算份數與價格。Yes—add multiple files; they're priced together.
Word 排版跑掉了My Word layout is messed up
Word 可免轉檔直接列印；若要排版完全一致，也可自行先轉成 PDF 再上傳。Word prints directly—no conversion needed; for an exact layout you may convert to PDF yourself first.
手機沒網路怎麼辦What if my phone has no internet?
掃機台 QR Code 上傳檔案時會自動連線傳檔，不需另外上網；其他用途請用手機行動網路。Scanning the QR auto-connects just to send your file—no internet needed; use mobile data for anything else.
上傳到一半想取消Cancel an upload in progress
直接關閉手機頁面即可取消，未付款不會列印也不會收費。Just close the page to cancel; nothing prints or charges before payment.
可以縮放或滿版嗎Can I scale or fit to page?
放大縮小提供少數固定倍率，也可選滿版或保留邊界，預覽會更新。Enlarge/reduce offers a few fixed ratios; you can also choose fit-to-page or margins—the preview updates.

系統會依頁數、黑白或彩色、紙張大小自動計價，付款前會顯示金額。Price is by pages, color, and size; shown before payment.

付款後通常幾秒到十幾秒就會出紙，份數多會久一點。Usually seconds after payment; more copies take longer.

您的檔案只暫存在店內主機處理，列印後會自動清除，不會上傳雲端。Your file is processed in-store and cleared after printing—never uploaded.
